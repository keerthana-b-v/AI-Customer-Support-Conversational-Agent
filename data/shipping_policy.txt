=========================================
GLOBAL E-COMMERCE RETAIL CO. - OFFICIAL DOMESTIC SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY
=========================================
Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Order Processing and Handling Time
All orders placed on our website are processed and prepared for shipping within twenty-four (24) business hours. Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST (Eastern Standard Time) Monday through Friday are usually dispatched from our fulfillment facility on the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST, on weekends, or during official federal holidays will be processed on the following business day. You will receive an automated shipping confirmation email containing your tracking number as soon as the carrier scans your package.

2. Domestic Shipping Tiers and Rates
We offer three main shipping methods for orders delivered within the contiguous United States:
* Standard Ground Shipping: Takes between three to five (3-5) business days for delivery. Standard shipping is a flat rate of $4.99 for all orders, or completely FREE if your order subtotal is fifty dollars ($50.00) or more before taxes and after any discount codes are applied.
* Expedited Shipping: Premium air/ground service that guarantees delivery within one to two (1-2) business days. Expedited shipping is available for a flat surcharge of $14.99 regardless of order value.
* Next-Day Air Shipping: Overnight delivery option available for orders placed before 12:00 PM EST on business days. Next-Day shipping is available for a flat surcharge of $29.99.

3. Geographic Coverage Restrictions
At this time, we only ship orders within the contiguous United States. We do not support shipping to:
* Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, Guam, or any other US Territories.
* APO/FPO/DPO military addresses due to complex customs clearance procedures.
* International destinations outside of the United States.
We are actively working on expanding our logistics network to support these regions in the future.

4. Package Tracking and Delivery Insurance
Every shipment is fully insured against loss, theft, or damage during transit at no additional cost to the customer. Once your order leaves our facility, you can monitor its progress using the tracking link sent via email. Please note that tracking numbers may take up to twenty-four (24) hours to show active tracking events on the carrier's portal. If your package tracking states "Delivered" but you have not received it, please check with neighbors, building management, or wait 24 hours as carriers sometimes mark packages as delivered prematurely.

5. Delayed Packages, Force Majeure, and Carrier Issues
While we do our best to ensure timely delivery, we cannot guarantee arrival dates in the event of carrier delays, severe weather conditions, natural disasters, sorting facility backlogs, or unexpected holiday peak season volume. If your package has been in transit for more than ten (10) business days beyond the estimated delivery date, please reach out to support@company.com, and we will coordinate with the carrier to initiate a missing package search or issue a replacement order.
