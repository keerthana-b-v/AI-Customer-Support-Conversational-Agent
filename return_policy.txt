1. Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase.
2. The item must be unused and in original packaging.
3. Customers must pay for return shipping unless the item arrived damaged.
4. Refunds take 5-7 business days to process back to the original credit card.
5. Clearance items are final sale and cannot be returned.
6. Electronics have a stricter 14-day return window and will incur a 15% restocking fee if the factory seal is broken.
7. Returns without a valid receipt or order number will only be issued store credit at the item's lowest selling price in the last 60 days.
8. Items purchased during major promotional events (e.g., Black Friday, Cyber Monday) are eligible for exchange only, and cannot be refunded for cash.
